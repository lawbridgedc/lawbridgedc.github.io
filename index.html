The main idea of the company

Our company focuses on people who have been forced to leave their country of residence as a result of illegal prosecution or pressure from government authorities. We are a team of specialists who coordinate activities and exchange information among lawyers, advocates, experts, and other specialists working in the interests and on behalf of the client in various fields, countries, and jurisdictions.

Our services and strategy

- Criminal defence in Russia, as well as in other countries where criminal proceedings are conducted, withoutthe need to return with the risk of arrest,
- Legal assistance in legalisation of stay in the host country (residence permit, asylum application or other),
- Legal assistance in matters of cooperation with Interpol. Requests for information on the presence or absenceof information on a person in the Interpol database, "preventive" requests to the Interpol Commission for FileControl in order to prevent the client's "exposure" to wanted databases, as well as requests to delete the “RedNotice”
- Legal assistance and representation in extradition proceedings in various countries,
- Legal opinions on the legality and validity of criminal proceedings, violations of the law and the rights andlegitimate interests of the client. The conclusions of the experts of this organisation are of great importance forthe objective legal position of the client and are taken into consideration by the European courts and theInterpol File Control Commission,
- The possibility of personal meetings with clients, regardless of their country of residence.
